The ingredients needed to make Vegan Shepherd's Pie:
  1. Get mashed potato layer
  2. Prepare 4 medium russet potatoes
  3. Take 1/4 cup butter
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup milk
  5. Prepare 2 clove garlic
  6. Take bottom layer
  7. Take 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  8. Make ready 1 large onion
  9. Take 3 cup mixed vegetables
  10. Prepare 1 clove garlic
  11. Make ready 1 cup hot chunky salsa
  12. Take 1 packages yves ground round
Instructions to make Vegan Shepherd's Pie:
  1. Bring potatoes to a boil until tender - 25 min - and drain
  2. Preheat oven to 400 and spray 2 qt baking dish with cooking spray
  3. Heat vegetable oil in large skillet, stir in onion, mixed vegetables and salsa.
  4. Crumble the veggie ground beef into skillet with vegetables - medium heat - about 10 min
  5. Spread vegetarian meat into bottom of baking dish cover with mashed potatoes
  6. Bake in preheated oven - about 20 min
