Instructions to make Fried yam with chicken sauce:
  1. Peel,wash and diced yam into strips and mix it with little salt
  2. Heat oil and fry yam strips until golden brown
  3. In another pan heat oil and add in diced onion and scotch bonnet
  4. Add in cooked chicken thigh and cube and stir occasionally
  5. Serve fried yam with chicken sauce. Enjoy!
