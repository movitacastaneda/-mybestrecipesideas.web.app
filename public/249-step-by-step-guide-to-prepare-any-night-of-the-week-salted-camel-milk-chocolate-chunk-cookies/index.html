The ingredients needed to make Salted Camel Milk Chocolate Chunk Cookies:
  1. Get 2 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  2. Prepare 1/4 teaspoon salt
  3. Take 1 teaspoon baking powder
  4. Get 1 teaspoon baking soda
  5. Get 2 teaspoons cornstarch
  6. Take 8 ounces unsalted butter, at room temperature (2 stickks)
  7. Get 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  8. Prepare 3/4 cup light brown sugar, packed
  9. Take 1 large egg and 2 large egg yolks
  10. Take 11/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  11. Take 3.5 ounce bar Lindor Exellence milk chocolate bar, broken into
  12. Take chunkd
  13. Make ready soft caramels such as Wgerthers Original soft caramels, unwrappe
  14. Take about 24 of them,. Soft caramels are needed for best results
  15. Make ready coarse sea salt for sprinkling
Instructions to make Salted Camel Milk Chocolate Chunk Cookies:
  1. Preheat the oven to 350. Line baking sheets with parchment paper
  2. In a bowl whisk together flour, baking soda,baking powder, salt and cornstarch
  3. In another bowl beat butter and both sugars until light and fluffy
  4. Add egg, yolks and vanilla, beat in
  5. Add flour mixture until just blended
  6. Fold I'm chocolate chunks
  8. Roll into 1 1/2 inch balls
  10. Push a caramel into dough ball and compleat enclose caraml
  12. Place 1 1/2 inches apart on prepated pans. Bake 11 to 14 minutes. For chewy cookies ne sure to not overcook. If you want crisp cookies cook a tiny bit more
  13. Sprinkle very lightly with sea salt as soon as they come out of the oven Cool in pans 3 minutes then transfer to racks to cool completely
