The ingredients needed to make Fried Bread Stuffed with Chicken and Vegetables:
  1. Take 12 slices bread
  2. Get 2 eggs
  3. Take 70 g bread crumbs
  4. Get 100 g potato
  5. Get 70 g mixed vegetables (carrot, corn, green pea)
  6. Prepare 100 g chicken fillet
  7. Get 150 ml milk
  8. Prepare 2 cloves garlic
  9. Get 50 g onion
  10. Prepare 1 scallion (take the green leaf)
  11. Take 2 Tbsp margarine/butter
  12. Take 1/2 Tsp mushroom stock powder
  13. Prepare 1 Tsp salt
  14. Prepare pinch black pepper

Instructions to make Fried Bread Stuffed with Chicken and Vegetables:
  1. Cut potato and chicken into small dice (1x1). Chop garlic and onion. Set aside
  2. Heat margarine/butter into the pan with medium heat. Put onion and garlic, stir until fragrance. Then add potatoes. Give small amount of water and cook for 5 minutes until half tender.
  3. Put chicken, salt, mushroom powder, pepper and cook until meat changes it's color. Then add mixed vegetables and scallion, stir it well.
  4. Pour milk into the pan and cook until thicken. Turn off the heat. Set aside
  5. Prepare bread slices, beaten egg and bread crumbs.
  6. Put the chicken filling on the middle of bread. Fold bread to triangle shape. Press bread edges with finger or fork to seal
  7. Put bread into beaten egg mixture, then cover it with bread crumbs. Do it until all ingredients run out. You can keep the bread in the fridge if you want to fry it later (put it inside zip bag first)
  8. Heat the oil with low fire. Fry the bread until it becomes golden brown
  9. Serve while it hot or put it on your lunch box ‚ú®
