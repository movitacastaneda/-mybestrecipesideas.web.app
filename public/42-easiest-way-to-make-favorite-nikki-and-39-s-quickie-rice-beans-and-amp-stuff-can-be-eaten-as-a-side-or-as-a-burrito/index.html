The ingredients needed to make Nikki's Quickie Rice, Beans & Stuff; can be eaten as a side or as a Burrito:
  1. Make ready 1 8 oz box yellow rice mix
  2. Make ready 2 cup chicken broth
  3. Make ready 1 15 oz can black beans, rinsed and drained
  4. Get 1 15 oz can corn, drained
  5. Take 1 14.5 oz can sweet onion flavor tomatoes, drained
  6. Prepare 1/4 cup chopped onion, optional
  7. Take 1 pack sazon
  8. Make ready 1/2 tsp black pepper
  9. Get 1/4 tsp onion powder
  10. Take 1 tbsp taco mix
  11. Prepare shredded cheese, (cheddar, taco, pepper jack, whatever you prefer) optional
  12. Get sour cream, optional
  13. Make ready 1 burrito style tortillas, if you're making it as burritos
  14. Prepare 1 cooked, shredded chicken breast, optional

Instructions to make Nikki's Quickie Rice, Beans & Stuff; can be eaten as a side or as a Burrito:
  1. cook rice according to directions on box, substituting broth for water, and add packet of sazon (before cooking). stir well and set aside.
  2. In a medium bowl, mix beans, corn, tomatoes, onions if using.spices, and taco mix. stir well and microwave 2-3 minutes.
  3. add cooked rice to bean mixture and stir well to incorporate.
  4. you can eat it just as is, with or without some chicken added in, or make a burrito with the mixture. top with cheese and serve with sour cream if desired.
  5. now enjoy your quick and easy meal!
