The ingredients needed to make Oreo cheesecake cups:
  1. Make ready 1 lb cream cheese (2 8 oz packs)
  2. Take 1/2 cup sugar
  3. Take 2 room temp eggs
  4. Get 1 tsp vanilla extract
  5. Get 1/2 cup sour cream
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  7. Get 22 oreos

Layers of Oreo cookies with cheesecake and fruit make a delicious summer treat. These easy Oreo Cheesecake Cookie Cups are the perfect treat for the Oreo lover in your life! Oreo cookie cups with an Oreo cheesecake filling. These Oreo Cheesecake Cookies are the ultimate Oreo lover's cookie.

Steps to make Oreo cheesecake cups:
  1. Preheat oven to 275
  2. Line cupcake tin with 16 liners
  3. Place an oreo in the bottom of each liner
  4. Mix cream cheese until smooth. Mix in sugar until well incorporated.
  5. Add one egg at a time and mix well.
  6. Mix in vanilla, sour cream, and salt.
  7. Roughly chop remaining oreos and fold into batter by hand.
  8. Fill each cupcake liner to the top with batter.
  9. Bake for 22-25 minutes or until the batter is set.
  10. Cool completely on baking rack and then refrigerate in tins for at least 4 hours.
