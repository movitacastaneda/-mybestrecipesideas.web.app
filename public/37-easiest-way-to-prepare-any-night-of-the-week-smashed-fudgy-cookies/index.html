The ingredients needed to make Smashed fudgy cookies:
  1. Prepare 2 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  2. Make ready 1 cup cocoa powder
  3. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  4. Take 2 tsp baking powder
  5. Get 1 cup brown sugar
  6. Make ready 2 eggs
  7. Make ready 1 cup melted butter
  8. Take 3 tbls vegetable oil
  9. Make ready 2 tsp vanilla extract
  10. Make ready 1/4 cup chopped cooking chocolate OR choc chip cookies

Instructions to make Smashed fudgy cookies:
  1. In a bowl, add the cocoa powder, sugar, butter, oil and vanilla extract. Mix well to combine
  2. Add each egg at a time and mix until everything is well incorporated
  3. In a separate bowl, sieve the flour, baking powder and salt and mix to combine.
  4. Fold in the flour mixture to the wet ingredients until a dough forms
  5. Fold in the chocolate pieces or choc chip
  6. Using a tablespoon, scoop some dough and form balls. Arrange the dough balls on an oven tray lined with parchment paper
  7. Using the same spoon, lightly press down the middle of the dough balls to spread them.
  8. Bake the cookies in a preheated oven for about 10-15 minutes at 190 degrees Celsius.
  9. Remove the cookies and allow to cool before serving. The cookies will be soft but will harden once they cool.
