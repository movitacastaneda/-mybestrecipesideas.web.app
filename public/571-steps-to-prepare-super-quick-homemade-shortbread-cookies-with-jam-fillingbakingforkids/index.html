The ingredients needed to make Shortbread Cookies With Jam Filling#BakingForKids:
  1. Get 2 cups all-purpose flour
  2. Make ready 1 cup unsalted butter or veg oil..I used veg oil
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup icing sugar
  4. Get 1/3 cup cornflour
  5. Make ready 1/2 cup milk
  6. Prepare 1 pinch salt
  7. Take Extra flour for dusting
  8. Get For the filling:
  9. Make ready Any kind of jam you fancy

Steps to make Shortbread Cookies With Jam Filling#BakingForKids:
  1. Get your ingredients ready. Preheat your oven to 180 degrees C
  2. Sift your dry ingredients
  3. Add in your butter or oil. I used a spatula to mix the oil and flour mixture. Add the milk little by little and form a dough
  4. Chill the dough for about 15 min-1 hour
  5. Pour a little flour on your working surface and roll out the dough to 1/2 cm thickness
  6. Cut out the 18 pieces. Using a small round cutter, a bottle top can do,punch small circles from 9 of the shortbread dough cuts.
  7. Place the cut dough on top of the cut dough and fill it with jam
  8. Place your cookies on your baking tray and bake for 15-20 min
  9. Ready to eat. Allow them to cool first
