The ingredients needed to make Chocolate chip cookie bars:
  1. Prepare 2 sticks butter or margarine
  2. Get 1 cup white sugar
  3. Take 1 cup packed brown sugar
  4. Take 2 eggs
  5. Get 3 cups ap flour
  6. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  7. Take 1 tsp baking powder
  8. Prepare 1 tsp baking soda
  9. Get Vanilla
  10. Prepare At least 1 cup or more chocolate chips

Steps to make Chocolate chip cookie bars:
  1. Preheat oven to 350. In mixer cream butter and both sugars til fluffy. Add eggs and vanilla and beat. Add salt, baking powder and baking soda to flour and add 1 cup at a time. Add chocolate chips.
  2. In a sprayed 9x13 pan spread mixture and bake about 30-40 minutes. Let cool and cut into squares.
