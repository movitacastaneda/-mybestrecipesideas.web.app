Steps to make Garlic bread with chicken soup:
  1. Add garlic paste, salt, pepper and coriander in the melted butter and mix them gently until well combined.
  2. Slice the bread into ur desired shapes or cut the corners of bread
  3. Now apply the butter mixture on the breads.
  4. Wrap the bread with foil and bake in a preheated oven at 180 C for about 2_3 minutes.
  5. For extra Toasted and crisp, remove the foil and bake for another 2 to 3 minutes and enjoy.
