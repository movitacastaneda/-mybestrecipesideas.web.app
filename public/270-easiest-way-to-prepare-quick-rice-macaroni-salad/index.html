The ingredients needed to make Rice Macaroni Salad:
  1. Make ready 1 cup macarona,boiled
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup cucumber,small cut
  3. Take 1/4 cup tomato,small cut
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup mint leaves and parsley,minced
  5. Prepare 1/4 cup aragula leaves,cut small
  6. Take 1/4 cup olive sliced green
  7. Get Sauce
  8. Prepare 1/2 pc Lemon
  9. Prepare 1-2 spoon Olive oil
  10. Make ready 1 spoon Pomegranates sauce
  11. Take Salt and black pepper
Instructions to make Rice Macaroni Salad:
  1. Wash all vegestables. And cut as desire.
  2. Boil the macaroni.
  3. In a bowl,put all ingredients and sauce then mix it good. Then put in salad bowl. Keep inside the refregirator to become cold before serve.
