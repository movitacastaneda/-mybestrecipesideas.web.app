  1. Preheat oven 375. Take chicken breast and filet them.

  4. Place a tbsp of cream cheese in the center with 2 to 3 jalape√±o on top of cream cheese. Drizzle some barbecue sauce on top of cream cheese.
  5. Fold chicken in half and wrap two pieces of bacon around to hold together.
  6. Place on a wire rack over a cooking pan so chicken grease will drip in pan.
  7. After placing all on wire rack drizzle barbecue sauce on top of bacon and place in oven for 25 minutes. Then for 2 to 3 minutes turn oven to broil to make bacon crispy on top. Enjoy…..
