The ingredients needed to make Keto chili dog bubble up:
  1. Get 28 oz can chili (lowest carb you can find or homemade)
  2. Make ready 8 hot dogs cut into bite size pieces
  3. Make ready 2 cups shredded cheddar
  4. Make ready 5 cups shredded mozzarella
  5. Get 4 oz cream cheese
  6. Take 2 tablespoon baking powder
  7. Take 6 eggs beaten
  8. Prepare 3 cups almond flour
  9. Prepare 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese

Steps to make Keto chili dog bubble up:
  1. Mix the chili and hot dogs and put in a 9x13 casserole and top with 1&1/2 cups of the shredded cheddar. Set aside.
  2. Melt the mozzarella and cream cheese in the microwave until completely melted. Stir as needed. Place the almond flour and baking powder in a bowl and stir until completely incorporated. When melted mix the cheeses into the almond flour. Add eggs slowly and mix until completely incorporated.
  3. Place grated parmesan in a shallow bowl. Using a tablespoon measure out the dough and drop the balls into the parmesan and roll around until covered a couple at a time. Place on top of the meat and cheese mixture as you go. Continue until all the dough is used, making little balls and dispersed as evenly as possible over the casserole.
