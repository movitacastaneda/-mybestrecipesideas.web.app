The ingredients needed to make Oatmeal Raisin Cookies:
  1. Get 2 Cups AP Flour SPOON FLOUR INTO MEASURING CUP
  2. Take 1/2 tsp baking soda
  3. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  4. Get 3/4 Cup unsalted, melted butter (slightly cooled)
  5. Get 1 Cup packed brown sugar
  6. Take 1/2 Cup white sugar
  7. Get 1 tsp cinnamon
  8. Get 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  9. Prepare 1 egg
  10. Get 1 egg yolk
  11. Make ready 1.5 Cup old fashioned oats
  12. Take 1 Cup raisins
  13. Get 1/2 cup chopped walnuts or pecans optional

Steps to make Oatmeal Raisin Cookies:
  1. Preheat oven to 325 and line baking sheets with parchment
  2. Mix flour, baking soda and salt
  3. In separate bowl mix butter, brown sugar and white sugar until well combined. Beat in cinnamon, vanilla, egg and egg yolk. Mix until creamy and light.
  4. Mix in flour until just blended. Stir in raisins and nuts and oats.
  5. Use 1/4 cup measurements and spoon mixture onto baking sheet. Bake 14-16 minutes. You're looking for brown edges but still soft in the middle.
