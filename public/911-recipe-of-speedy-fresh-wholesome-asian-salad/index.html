The ingredients needed to make Fresh wholesome Asian salad:
  1. Prepare 1 head lettuce (I use red leaf)
  2. Make ready 1 cucumber
  3. Prepare 2 carrots
  4. Take 1/2 bag of baby spinach
  5. Prepare 1/2 head red cabbage
  6. Prepare Garnish
  7. Get 1 chow mein noodles
  8. Take 1 sliced almonds
  9. Make ready 1 (optional) green onion
  10. Make ready dressing
  11. Get 1 (I recommend buying a pre-made Asian dressing to save time and money)
  12. Get 3 clove garlic, minced
  13. Take 2 tbsp fresh ground ginger root
  14. Get 3/4 cup olive oil
  15. Prepare 1/3 cup rice vinegar
  16. Prepare 1/2 cup soy sauce
  17. Prepare 3 tbsp honey
  18. Make ready 1/4 cup water

Steps to make Fresh wholesome Asian salad:
  1. Chop carrots and cucumber
  2. Rinse lettuce, spinach and cabbage. Chop to preferred size
  3. Mix all vegetables in a giant bowl, garnish with almonds, chow mein noodles and dressing.
  4. FOR DRESSING:
  5. In a 1 pint glass jar or larger, combine the garlic, ginger, olive oil, rice vinegar, soy sauce, honey, and water. Cover the jar with a tight fitting lid, and shake well. Remove lid, and heat jar in the microwave for 1 minute just to dissolve the honey. Let cool, and shake well before serving. Store covered in the refrigerator.
