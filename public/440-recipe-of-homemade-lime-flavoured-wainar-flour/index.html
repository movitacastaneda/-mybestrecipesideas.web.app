Steps to make Lime flavoured wainar flour:
  1. Wash up pepper and onions, on a chopping board chop into small pieces giving it a facade of being grated.
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, water, seasoning, salt, chopped onions and pepper.
  3. Slice onions into thin lines, in a pan saute it with lime juice and add to the flour mixture. Mix until smooth.
  4. Put a nonstick pan on medium heat, grease the pan with butter or oil and add the batter in little quantities and spread through out the pan. the thinner it is, the better it tastes.
  5. Serve with regular ground dry pepper.
