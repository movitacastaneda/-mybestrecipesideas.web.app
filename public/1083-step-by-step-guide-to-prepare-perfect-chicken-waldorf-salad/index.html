The ingredients needed to make chicken waldorf salad:
  1. Prepare 3/4 cup mayonaise
  2. Get 2 tsp lemon juice
  3. Prepare 1/4 tsp salt
  4. Prepare 1 dash pepper
  5. Take 1 cup green grapes (cut up or sliced)
  6. Make ready 1 cup chopped apple
  7. Get 1 stock slices or chopped celery
  8. Make ready 1 cup chopped walnuts
  9. Take 1 1/2 cup shredded or diced already cooked chicken breast
