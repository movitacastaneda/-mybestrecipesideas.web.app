Steps to make Homemade fresh sardines:
  1. Peel potatoes skin & cut potatoes in cube then fried. Set aside
  2. Cut sardines heads and tails, cut to half, clean stomach
  3. Cut tomatoes into cube (easy to melt)
  4. Oil in frying pan, warm enough enter garlic, onion, and chili until yellow
  5. Enter tomatoes medium heat until melt like sauce
  6. Enter salt, ajinomoto, chili powder, black pepper, and white pepper
  7. Enter sardines and cook until sauce absorbed into fish with medium heat
  8. Lastly enter fried potatoes and ready to serve
