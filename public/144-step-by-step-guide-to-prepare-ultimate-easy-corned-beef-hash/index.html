The ingredients needed to make Easy corned beef hash:
  1. Make ready 1 medium yellow onion-diced
  2. Make ready 2 lb corned beef (brisket or round), cooked and cubed 1/4" dice
  3. Get 4 cup new potatoes-1" cubed and boiled
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup canola or veg oil
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  6. Make ready 1/3 tsp salt
  7. Get 1/2 tsp granulated garlic

Instructions to make Easy corned beef hash:
  1. heat large non-stick or cast iron skillet or griddle over medium-high heat. add 1/2 of the oil and add onion. cook for 2-3 minutes, until onions begin to soften. add potatoes and cook another 5-6 minutes, stirring often.
  2. add corned beef and continue to stir occasionally, 4 or 5 minutes until heated through.
  3. season to taste.
