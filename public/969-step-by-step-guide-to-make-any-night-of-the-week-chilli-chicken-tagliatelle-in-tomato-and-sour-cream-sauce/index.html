The ingredients needed to make Chilli Chicken Tagliatelle in Tomato and Sour Cream Sauce:
  1. Prepare Chilli Coated Chicken
  2. Take 2 Sliced Chicken Breasts
  3. Prepare 1/3 tsp Paprika
  4. Take 1/4 tsp Red Cayenne Pepper (Chilli Powder)
  5. Prepare 1 tsp Salt
  6. Prepare 1 pinch Ground White Pepper
  7. Make ready 1 tsp Garlic Infused Olive Oil
  8. Prepare 4 slice Streaky Bacon
  9. Take Tomato and Sour Cream Sauce
  10. Prepare 1 can Chopped Tomatoes
  11. Get 1 pinch Ground Black Pepper
  12. Prepare 1 pinch Salt
  13. Prepare 1 bunch Chopped Coriander

  21. Take 1250 ml water
Instructions to make Chilli Chicken Tagliatelle in Tomato and Sour Cream Sauce:
  1. Start by adding the cayenne pepper, paprika, salt and white pepper to a bowl and mix to make a uniform powder.
  2. Add the garlic oil to the Chilli mix
  3. Mix in the chicken and bacon to get an even coating then leave the bowl to one side.
  4. Bring a saucepan of water to a rolling boil, add a pinch of salt and then the pasta and cook for 8 minutes. (Test texture around 6-7 minutes, it should be soft with some springiness when done).
  5. Add the chopped tomatoes to a medium heat saucepan and simmer.
  6. Add the Coriander, Garlic, Chilli and Tomato Pur√©e and continue to simmer stirring occasionally. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Bring a frying pan to a high heat with a dash of oil and add the Chilli Chicken mix.
  8. Fry the chicken and bacon, searing on each side before turning.
  9. Once the chicken is sealed add the sauce to the frying pan and turn the heat to medium-low. Cook until chicken is cooked through.
  10. Once the pasta has the right texture, drain the water in a colander without drying the pasta completely.
  11. Add the pasta, chicken and sauce to the same saucepan and put on a low heat.
  12. Finally, add the sour cream whilst stirring and black pepper to taste. Serve with garlic bread if you wish!
