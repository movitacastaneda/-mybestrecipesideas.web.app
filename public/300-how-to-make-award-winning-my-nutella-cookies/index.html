The ingredients needed to make My Nutella Cookies:
  1. Take 1 1/2 cup all purpose flour
  2. Take 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp corn starch
  4. Prepare 3/4 tsp baking soda
  5. Prepare 1/4 tsp salt
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup soften
  7. Take 3/4 cup packed light brown sugar or dark
  8. Get 1/2 cup nutella
  9. Take 1 tsp vanilla
  10. Get 1 large egg
  11. Get 3/4 cup chocolate chips

Instructions to make My Nutella Cookies:
  1. In a large bowl whisk together the flour, cocoa, cornstarch, baking soda and salt. Set aside.
  2. In a separate large bowl, beat together the butter and sugar until fluffy. Beat in the Nutella, followed by the egg and vanilla.
  3. Turn the mixer down to low and add in the flour mixture about 1/2 at a time.
  4. Turn off the mixer and scrape down the sides of the bowl using a large wooden spoon or rubber spatula. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  5. When ready to bake, preheat the oven to 350F degrees.
  6. Form the cookies into balls about 1 - 1.5 tablespoons in size (I recommend using a cookie scoop), and place the cookies 2 inches apart on the lined cookie sheets.
  7. Bake 1 tray at a time in the preheated oven for 11-13 minutes, or until the tops look set.
  8. Remove from the oven and allow the cookies to cool fully on the cookie tray. Optionally, dot the tops of each cookie with extra chocolate chips.
