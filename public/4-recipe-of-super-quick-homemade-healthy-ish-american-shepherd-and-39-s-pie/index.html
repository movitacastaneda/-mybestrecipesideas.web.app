The ingredients needed to make Healthy-ish American Shepherd's Pie:
  1. Prepare 2 head of Cauliflower
  2. Prepare 1 tbsp Milk
  3. Take 2 tbsp Butter
  4. Take 2 lb Ground Turkey or Chicken
  5. Get 1 Onion
  6. Get 25 small Carrots, or so
  7. Get Garlic (Powder or Fresh)
  8. Prepare 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  9. Get 1/2 cup Worcestershire Sauce
  10. Get 1/2 can Beef or Chicken Broth
  11. Prepare 1 cup Shredded Cheese (I tried Mozz this time.)
  12. Prepare Salt and Pepper
  13. Prepare 2 tablespoons flour
Steps to make Healthy-ish American Shepherd's Pie:
  1. Start the cauliflower mash. Boil a pot of water then add cauliflower. Let cook approx 10-15 minutes, or until soft.
  2. Start Meat. SautÃ© with oil: onion and carrots, then garlic. Add meat, and lightly brown. Add flour, then Worcestershire sauce and broth. Add salt and pepper as needed.
  3. You can add green beans and corn to meat pan.
  4. Drain cauliflower, add milk, butter and salt and pepper as needed. Blend in blender until reaches mashed potato -like consistency.
  5. Grease 2-3 qt casserole dish. Add layer of meat, then layer of cheese, then cauliflower mash on top. Make sure cauliflower mash covers to the sides so meat does not boil out and cause a mess. Add a dash of paprika on top.
  6. Bake at 400Â°F for 25-30 minutes. Let cool for 10 mins.
