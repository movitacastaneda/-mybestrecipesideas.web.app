Steps to make Keto Crack Chicken with Cauliflower Rice:
  1. Chop up peppers and onions and add to crock pot
  2. Pour chicken broth and reanch seasoning into crock pot and stir up
  3. Add chicken breast to crock pot. Cover and cook on high for 4 hours
  4. Remove chicken from pot and shred with forks then return to crock pot
  5. Add bacon bits, cream cheese and cheddar to crock pot and stir up.
  6. Cover and cook an additional 10 minutes or until cheeses melt
  7. Cook cauliflower in microwave and scoop into bottom of all portion containers
  8. Top cauliflower with crack Chicken from crock pot
  9. Enjoy
