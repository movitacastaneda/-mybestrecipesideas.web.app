The ingredients needed to make Mojito Lime Grilled Chicken:
  1. Get 4 piece Chicken
  2. Prepare 5 garlic cloves, minced
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup onion, chopped
  4. Get 1/4 cup Orange juice
  5. Get 1/4 cup Lime juice
  6. Prepare 1/4 cup canola oil
  7. Prepare 3 tsp Salt & Pepper
  8. Make ready 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  9. Prepare 1 tsp oregano
  10. Make ready 1 1/2 tbsp cilantro, chopped
  11. Take 1/4 tsp chili powder (Cheyenne)
