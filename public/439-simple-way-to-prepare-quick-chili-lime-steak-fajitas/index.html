The ingredients needed to make Chili Lime Steak Fajitas:
  1. Get 6 tortillas
  2. Prepare 1.75 lbs. Flank Steak, thinly sliced
  3. Make ready 3 green peppers, sliced
  4. Take 1 yellow onion, sliced
  5. Prepare Marinade:
  6. Make ready 2 Tbsp. Olive oil
  7. Take 1/3 C. Freshly squeezed lime juice
  8. Prepare 2 Tbsp. Cilantro, chopped
  9. Prepare 2 cloves garlic, minced
  10. Prepare 1 tsp. Brown sugar
  11. Make ready 3/4 tsp. Red Chili flakes
  12. Get 1/2 tsp. Ground cumin
  13. Prepare 1 tsp. Salt
  14. Get Creamy Cilantro Lime Sauce:
  15. Take 2 C. Greek yoghurt
  16. Take Zest of 1 lime
  17. Get Juice of 1 lime
  18. Get 2 Tbsp olive oil
  19. Get 1/4 C. Cilantro, chopped
  20. Take 1 tsp. Salt
  21. Take 1/2 tsp. Sugar
Steps to make Chili Lime Steak Fajitas:
  1. Whisk marinade ingredients together to combine. Pour half of the marinade into a bowl and add steak. Marinade for 30 minutes or overnight. Remove 30 minutes prior to cooking if refrigerated overnight. Reserve remaining marinade for use later.
  2. Heat 1 tsp. of oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat and cook steak until cooked through. Stirring occasionally. Set aside and let rest.
  3. Wipe pan and drizzle with 1 Tbsp. Olive oil. Saute peppers and onion until almost tender. Add in reserved marinade and salt, pepper and red pepper flakes and continue cooking until soft and translucent. Add back in the sliced steak.
  4. For the Cilantro Lime Sauce combine all ingredients in a small. Owl and stir to combine. Or mix in a blender until all ingredients are incorporated.
  5. In a medium dry skillet over medium-high heat char tortillas on each side for 1-2 minutes.
  6. Assemble fajitas. Spoon steak and veggie mixture into charred tortillas. Top with cilantro lime sauce and sprinkle with cilantro.
