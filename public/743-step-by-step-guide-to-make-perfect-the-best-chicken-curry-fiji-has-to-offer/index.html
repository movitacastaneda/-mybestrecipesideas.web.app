The ingredients needed to make The best chicken curry Fiji has to offer!!:
  1. Make ready 2 large chicken breasts (cubed)
  2. Get 1 teaspoon black mustard seed
  3. Take 1 tablespoon curry powder (NOT PASTE!!)
  4. Make ready 1 large white potato (cubed)
  5. Take 1/2 carrot (sliced)
  6. Take 1 teaspoon masala (red)
  7. Make ready Rice
  8. Prepare 1 white onion (diced)
  9. Make ready 2 tablespoon olive oil

Steps to make The best chicken curry Fiji has to offer!!:
  1. In a large pot, add olive oil and spices
  2. Mix until fragrant
  3. Add onion and let simmer until lightly browned
  4. Add potatoes and chicken
  5. Cook until chicken is tender and potatoes are soft
  6. Eat with rice
  7. Enjoy!
