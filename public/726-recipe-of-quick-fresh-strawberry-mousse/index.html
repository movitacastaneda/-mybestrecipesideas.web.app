The ingredients needed to make Fresh Strawberry Mousse:
  1. Prepare 2 quarts fresh strawberries, tops removed, sliced
  2. Get 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice
  3. Take 1 (8 teaspoon) salt
  4. Prepare 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice
  5. Prepare 1 (8 teaspoon) salt
  6. Get 1 3 oz box strawberry jello
  7. Make ready 2 cups cold heavy whipping cream
  8. Make ready 1 st teaspoon vanilla extract
  9. Get 1/4 cups confectioners sugar
  10. Take For Garnish
  11. Prepare Fresh whipped cream and grated chocolate
Instructions to make Fresh Strawberry Mousse:
  1. Purée strawberries, lemon and salt in a food processor. Strain through a fine mesh strainer.
  2. Heat one cup straw juice to a boil. Add jello and stir to dissolve completely. Add 1 cup cold strawberry juice and refrigerate until cold bit Not setting up_
  3. Beat cream until starting to thucken. Add vanilla and sugar. Beat to just hold its shape
  4. Fold un cold strawberry mixture m spoon into glassed and refrigerate at least 6 hours or overnight
  5. Garnish with whipped cream and chocolate shavings
