The ingredients needed to make Oreo Cheesecake:
  1. Take 5 packs of Oreos
  2. Prepare 50 grams butter
  3. Get 300 ml whipping cream
  4. Prepare 250 grams Cream cheese
  5. Get 1/2 cup icing sugar

Instructions to make Oreo Cheesecake:
  1. Crush 4 packets of Oreos
  2. Melt butter and pour over crushed Oreos and mix well.
  3. Press into a round springform tin and refrigerate.
  4. Put cream, cream cheese, icing sugar and last packet of crushed Oreos into a bowl.
  5. Beat until thick with a hand mixer.
  6. Pour over Oreo base and sprinkle with more Oreos if desired. Refrigerate for a few hours.
  7. ENJOY
