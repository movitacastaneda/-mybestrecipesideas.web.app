The ingredients needed to make Mike's Adult Lemon Lime Slushies:
  1. Take 1 oz Quality Vodka [per small glass]
  2. Get as needed Ice Cubes
  3. Get 3 LG Lemon Juiced [1 zested]
  4. Take 2 Small Lime Juiced [1 zested]
  5. Prepare 1/2 Cup Sugar [or more to taste]
  6. Make ready as needed Water
Steps to make Mike's Adult Lemon Lime Slushies:
  1. Add ice, water, sugar and vodka to blender.
  2. Zest your lime and lemon.
  3. Juice your lime and lemons. If you don't have a lemon or lime juicer - simply heat fruits in the microwave for 30 seconds, roll on the counter with your palm, cut open and squeeze.
  4. Blend well.
  5. Garnish drinks with lime and lemon wedges. Enjoy!
