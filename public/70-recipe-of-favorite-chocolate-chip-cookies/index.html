The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Chip Cookies:
  1. Make ready 1 C. Salted butter, softened
  2. Get 1 C. Granulated sugar
  3. Make ready 1 C. Light brown sugar, packed
  4. Prepare 2 tsp. Pure vanilla extract
  5. Get 2 large eggs
  6. Get 3 C. All-purpose flour
  7. Get 1 tsp. Baking soda
  8. Prepare 1/2 tsp. Baking powder
  9. Make ready 1 tsp. Sea salt
  10. Make ready 2 C. Chocolate chips or chunks or chopped chocolate

Instructions to make Chocolate Chip Cookies:
  1. Preheat oven to 375°F. Lightly grease a baking pan and set aside.
  2. In a large bowl mix flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt. Set aside.
  3. Cream together the butter and sugars until combined.
  4. Beat in eggs and vanilla until fluffy.
  5. Mix in the dry ingredients until combined.
  6. Add in the chocolate chips and mix well.
  7. Scoop into 1 Tbsp. heaps about 2" apart of baking pan. Bake 8-10 minutes until lightly brown. Remove to cooling rack.
