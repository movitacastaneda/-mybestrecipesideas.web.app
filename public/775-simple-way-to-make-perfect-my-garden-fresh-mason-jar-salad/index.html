The ingredients needed to make My Garden Fresh Mason Jar Salad:
  1. Get 2-3 tbsp. your favorite dressing
  2. Take 1/4 cup sliced grape tomatoes
  3. Get 1 medium carrot, diced or julienned
  4. Take 1/4 cup diced red onions
  5. Take 1/4 cup sliced radishes
  6. Make ready 1/4 cup brocoli florets (cut small)
  7. Get 1 hard boiled egg, diced (optional)
  8. Make ready 1/2 chicken breast, cooked, cooled and diced (optional)
  9. Take 2 tbsp. cheese of choice (shredded, cubed or crumbled)
  10. Make ready 1 cup shredded purple cabbage
  11. Prepare 2 cups lettuce of choice (pictured is romaine)
  12. Prepare 1 tbsp. pepitas (optional)
  13. Get 1 tsp. sunflower seeds (optional)
  14. Make ready wide mouthed quart sized mason jar with lid

Steps to make My Garden Fresh Mason Jar Salad:
  1. Start by placing your dressing in the bottom of the mason jar. Pictured is my homemade ranch, but a vinaigrette will spread easier when you go to eat. My favorite to use generally is my homemade honey mustard vinaigrette. Next come the veggies that can hold up to soaking in the dressing. Place in the tomatoes, carrots, onions, radishes and broccoli florets, in that order.
  2. Next, if you are using the diced, hard boiled egg or the diced chicken (or both) then place them in now.
  3. Next comes the cheese, cabbage and lettuce. You may need to pack the lettuce in gently. At the very top, place the pepitas and sunflower seeds (if using). You could also place croutons in instead.
  4. Place the lid on and refrigerate until you're ready to eat. Then, either shake it up and eat straight from the jar or dump everything into a bowl and toss around before eating.
