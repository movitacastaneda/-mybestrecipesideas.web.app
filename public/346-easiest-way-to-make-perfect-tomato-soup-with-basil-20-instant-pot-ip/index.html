The ingredients needed to make Tomato soup with basil 2.0 instant pot ip:
  1. Get 2 tablespoons olive oil
  2. Get 1 small yellow onion diced
  3. Get 3 celery stalks diced with leaves
  4. Make ready 1 red pepper diced, seeds and membrane removed
  5. Get 6 baby carrots diced
  6. Take 4 cloves garlic minced
  7. Get 2 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  8. Take 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  9. Take 1 cup fresh basil chopped
  10. Get to taste Fresh ground pepper
  11. Get 1 (28 oz) can diced tomatoes
  12. Get 1/2 cup sherry
  13. Make ready 1 cup water
  14. Take 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  15. Make ready 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
  16. Make ready Garnishes: Shredded mild cheddar cheese, croutons

Instructions to make Tomato soup with basil 2.0 instant pot ip:
  1. Turn on instant pot to saute high. When hot add olive oil and onions, carrots, red pepper, and celery. Cook about 10 minutes stirring frequently. Add garlic cook for another minute.
  2. Add broth, sherry, tomatoes with juice, salt, half the basil, water, and tomato paste. Stir to combine making sure there is no bits on the bottom of pot.
  3. Seal pressure cooker and cook at manual (high pressure) for 5 minutes. Then quick release the pressure.
  4. After removing lid use a immersion blender to mix and puree till smooth. Or puree in blender in batches.
  5. Stir in heavy whipping cream.
  6. Ladle into bowls and top with croutons, cheddar cheese, and basil.
  7. Enjoy!
