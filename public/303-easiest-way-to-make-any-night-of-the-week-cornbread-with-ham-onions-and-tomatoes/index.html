The ingredients needed to make Cornbread with Ham, Onions and Tomatoes:
  1. Make ready 1/2 pound smoked ham
  2. Take 1/4 cup lard
  3. Take 15 ounces canned diced tomatoes with their juices
  4. Prepare 1-1/2 tablespoons chives
  5. Get 1 tablespoon green onion
  6. Get 1-1/2 cups buttermilk cornbread mix
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup self rising flour
  8. Prepare 1 teaspoon double acting baking powder
  9. Get 1 cup water
  10. Make ready 1/3 cup finely chopped bell pepper
  11. Make ready 1 large egg
  12. Take 1 teaspoon salt
  13. Take 1 tablespoon sugar

Instructions to make Cornbread with Ham, Onions and Tomatoes:
  1. Get the ham that has the least amount of white streaks in it. Remove the white tough membrane. Chop the onions, chives, and bell pepper. Mince the ham.
  2. Mix the lard, flour, cornbread mix, egg, sugar, baking powder, pepper, onion mixture, ham, and salt. Add the tomato and its juices. Then finally stir in the water.
  3. Heat the lard it will smoke, it will burn, if you're not careful. Spoon in a clump and fry. Turn often or it will char. Pile them on a paper towel till all are done. I hope you enjoy!!
