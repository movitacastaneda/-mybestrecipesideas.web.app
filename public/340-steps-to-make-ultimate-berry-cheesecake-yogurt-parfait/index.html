The ingredients needed to make Berry Cheesecake Yogurt Parfait:
  1. Take 1/2 cup Greek yogurt (vanilla or plain)
  2. Take 1 tbsp. cream cheese
  3. Take 1 tsp. honey or maple syrup
  4. Get 1/2 cup granola or muesli
  5. Get 1 small Graham cracker square, crumbled
  6. Take 1/2 cup mixed berries of choice

Steps to make Berry Cheesecake Yogurt Parfait:

  2. Layer the granola, Graham crackers and berries over the top of the yogurt mix.
