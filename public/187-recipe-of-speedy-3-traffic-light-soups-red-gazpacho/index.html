The ingredients needed to make 3 Traffic Light Soups - RED - Gazpacho:
  1. Make ready 2 Cups Plumb Tomatoes
  2. Prepare 0.5 Cup White Onion
  3. Prepare 1 Cup Cucumber - Peeled
  4. Take to taste Basil - Fresh - I use about 0.5 Cup as I like it
  5. Prepare 3 tbsp Lime or Lemon Juice - Freshly Squeezed
  6. Prepare to taste Tabasco - I use 1 Tbles
  7. Prepare 3 Tbsp Olive Oil
  8. Make ready 1 Tsp Salt
  9. Make ready 2 Tsp Fresh Ground Black Pepper
  10. Take 1 Tbsp Sugar
  11. Get 1 Garlic Clove
Steps to make 3 Traffic Light Soups - RED - Gazpacho:
  1. Chop All the Veg and put 80% into the Blender with all the other ingredients and blend until smooth.
  2. Add the remaining 20% of veg and pulse 2-3 times so you have a nice chunky soup.
  3. Chill (over night best)
  4. You can add a little water if too thick, I also love to add a little Vodka if a special occation.
