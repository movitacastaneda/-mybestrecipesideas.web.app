The ingredients needed to make Keto Beef Suet Soup:
  1. Make ready 1/2 cup cubed beef fat
  2. Take 1 scrambled egg
  3. Prepare bokchoy
  4. Get 1/2 tbsp coco aminos
  5. Take 1 tsp fish sauce
  6. Make ready 2 cups beef broth
  7. Take Minced garlic
  8. Prepare Minced onion
  9. Get 1 tsp minced ginger
  10. Make ready Salt & pepper
Steps to make Keto Beef Suet Soup:
  1. In a pot over medium heat saute ginger onion and garlic for 1 min.
  2. Add beef fat, coco aminos, fish sauce, and beef broth. Bring to boil and cook for atleast 20 mins. Then add the bokchoy. Add salt and pepper to taste
  3. In a frying pan over medium heat, pour a small amount of egg so it will be thin. Make sure it is spread in the pan like youre making a crepe. Cook both sides for 1 min each
  4. Roll the egg and cut it vertically it should look like this
