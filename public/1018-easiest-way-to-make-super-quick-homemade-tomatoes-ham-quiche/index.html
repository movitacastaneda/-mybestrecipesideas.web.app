Instructions to make Tomatoes, ham quiche:
  1. Heat oven to 425
  2. Combine thawed hash browns with bell pepper, melted butter salt and pepper to taste
  3. Press hash browns into baking dish bake 25 min
  4. Mix eggs, cheese, soup, onions, salt and pepper to taste
  5. Remove hash browns from oven, place sliced tomatoes on hash browns cover with egg mixture
  6. Reduce heat in oven to 350 bake for 45 to 55 minutes slice and enjoy
  7. You can top with a tablespoon of sour cream optional
