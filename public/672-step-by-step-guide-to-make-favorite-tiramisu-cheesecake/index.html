Steps to make Tiramisu Cheesecake:
  1. Mix A until smooth
  2. Pour B slowly, keep mixing until incorporated
  3. Pour C, mix with spatula slowly
  4. Divide into 3 round baking pans of 26cm diameter. Bake in 200 celsius for 15 minutes
  5. D: mix cream cheese until smooth, pour other ingredients, mix evenly
  6. Spread cream cheese over the cake, garnish with cocoa powder, strawberry or chocolate bar
