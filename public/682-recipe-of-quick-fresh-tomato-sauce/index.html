The ingredients needed to make Fresh tomato Sauce:
  1. Prepare 4-5 large fresh heirloom tomatoes, chopped
  2. Make ready 2-3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  3. Take 1/2 tsp sea salt or to taste
  4. Take 2 cloves garlic crushed then rough chopped
  5. Take 1/4 c fresh basil or basil and flat parsley mix
  6. Get Fresh fettuccini or spinach tagliatele
  7. Make ready Grated parmesan cheese

Instructions to make Fresh tomato Sauce:
  1. Saute tomatoes and olive oil in a saute pan on medium heat for 10-15 minutes, stirring on occasion.
  2. Add garlic, salt and herbs. Stir, lower heat and simmer for another 10-15 minutes. Depending on how much moisture is in the tomatoes you may need to add some water to the sauce if needed.
  3. Boil several quarts of water in a large stock pot. Add a large pinch of salt to the water as it nears a boil. Cook pasta as directed. Take care not to over cook fresh pasta. Strain and return to stick pot.
  4. Place pasta in pasta bowls, top with tomato sauce and serve with grated parmesan cheese.
