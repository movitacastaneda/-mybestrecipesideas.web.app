The ingredients needed to make Homemade fresh egg noodles:
  1. Get 2 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  2. Take 1/2 tsp salt
  3. Get 2 eggs beaten
  4. Take 1/2 cup milk
  5. Get 1 tbsp melted butter

HOMEMADE FRESH EGG NOODLE is made with flour, egg, salt, water and oil. It is easier than you may think, with just this simple ingredients you can have that. When my mom told me that I could make my own egg noodles, I found it hard to believe. Sift together the flour, salt and baking powder.

Steps to make Homemade fresh egg noodles:
  1. Mix first flour then salt. Then eggs, milk and butter.
  2. Knead the dough on a broad that's heavily floured. Until smooth about 5 minutes.
  3. Then cover the dough balled for 10 minutes.
  4. Roll out on board to 1/4 inch. And slice to your preference. I used a pizza cutter.
  5. Stack and air dry. About 15 minutes. They should be soft and not sticky.
  6. Cook for 2 to 3 minutes. Place in already boiling salted water. Use a big pot so they don't stick together.

Add egg yolks and mix until dry ingredients are moistened. Press into a ball and cut in quarters. Homemade Egg Noodles with four simple ingredients and no special equipment.
